Track. Reduce. Sustain.

AI-powered carbon emissions tracking for delivery fleets. Real-time GPS monitoring, driver analytics, and intelligent insights — all in one app.

The Problem

India's logistics sector emits millions of tonnes of CO₂ — with zero accountability

Most fleet operators have no visibility into their carbon footprint. Drivers get no feedback. Owners can't identify which vehicles are most polluting.

📊

No Data

Fleet operators have zero visibility into per-vehicle or per-driver emissions data.

🚗

No Feedback

Drivers receive no real-time guidance on idling, overspeeding, or fuel efficiency.

🏭

No Accountability

Without measurement, there's no way to set targets, track progress, or reduce emissions.

💸

Wasted Fuel

Excessive idling and inefficient driving waste thousands of rupees in fuel every month.

Features

Everything a fleet needs to go green

📍

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Stream-based location updates every 10 metres. Smart drift filtering prevents false distance accumulation when stationary.

🌿

Accurate CO₂ Calculation

Formula accounts for distance, fuel type, idle time, and engine efficiency. A less efficient engine means higher emissions.

🤖

AI-Powered Insights

GPT-4o-mini generates per-trip recommendations, efficiency scores, money savings estimates, and real-time coaching tips every 2 minutes.

🏢

Fleet Owner Dashboard

Total fleet CO₂ by day/week/month/year. Per-driver breakdown table. Overspeeding alerts with exact date, time, and speed.

💬

AI Fleet Assistant

Owner can chat with an AI that has full access to fleet data — ask about top emitters, overspeeding incidents, or savings opportunities.

🔐

Secure Authentication

Phone OTP via Twilio or email OTP via Supabase. Role-based access: drivers see their trips, owners see the full fleet.

🇮🇳

Hindi Language Support

Full English / Hindi toggle. All UI text, buttons, and AI prompts switch language instantly.

⏸️

Driver Break Detection

Drivers can pause tracking during breaks. Break time is tracked separately and excluded from idle time calculations.

🏎️

Speedometer & Max Speed

Live speed display with colour-coded bar. Max speed recorded per trip and flagged if over 120 km/h in the owner dashboard.

How It Works

Two roles. One app.

🚛 For Drivers

1

Login via phone OTP or email

2

Search for your company and send a join request

3

Set fuel type, load weight, engine efficiency

4

Start Trip — GPS tracks distance, speed, idle time automatically

5

Take breaks without affecting idle time

6

Stop Trip — get CO₂ result, efficiency score, and AI recommendations

7

View history and personal dashboard with trends

🏢 For Fleet Owners

1

Login and create your company

2

Review driver join requests and approve them

3

Monitor total fleet CO₂ across Day / Week / Month / Annual

4

See per-driver CO₂ table with truck numbers

5

Review overspeeding alerts with driver, date, time, and speed

6

Read AI-generated fleet insights and recommendations

7

Chat with the AI assistant for instant fleet analysis
