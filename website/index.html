The Problem

India's logistics sector emits millions of tonnes of CO₂ — with zero accountability

Most fleet operators have no visibility into their carbon footprint. Drivers get no feedback. Owners can't identify which vehicles are most polluting.

📊

No Emissions Data

Fleet operators have zero visibility into per-vehicle or per-driver CO₂ output.

🚗

No Driver Feedback

Drivers receive no real-time guidance on idling, overspeeding, or fuel efficiency.

🏭

No Accountability

Without measurement, there's no way to set targets, track progress, or reduce emissions.

💸

Wasted Fuel

Excessive idling and inefficient driving waste thousands of rupees in fuel every month.

How It Works

Two roles. One app. Complete fleet visibility.

🚛 Driver Flow

1

Login via phone OTP (SMS) or email OTP

2

First time: fill profile — name, DOB, location, truck number, phone (optional)

3

Search for your company by name → send join request to owner

4

Wait on pending screen until owner accepts your request

5

Set fuel type (diesel/petrol), load weight (kg), engine efficiency (km/L)

6

Start Trip — GPS stream begins, live dashboard shows speed, distance, idle time, ignition time

7

Break button — pause tracking during rest stops, break time tracked separately

8

Stop Trip — data sent to backend, AI calculates CO₂ and generates insights

9

Result screen — CO₂ emitted, efficiency score, money savings, AI recommendations

10

Dashboard — view personal trip history and CO₂ trends by day/week/month

🏢 Fleet Owner Flow

1

Login via phone OTP or email OTP

2

First time: fill profile — name, DOB, location, company name (creates the company)

3

Requests tab — review pending driver join requests, Accept or Reject each one

4

Analytics tab — total fleet CO₂ for Day / Week / Month / Annual with colour-coded rating

5

Drivers tab — table showing every driver's name, truck number, location, and CO₂ for selected period

6

Speed tab — overspeeding log with driver name, date, time, max speed, truck number

7

AI Insights — auto-generated fleet analysis with 3 actionable recommendations

8

AI Assistant — floating chat button, ask anything about your fleet in natural language

9

Profile page — view company info, switch to driver view (dev mode), logout

AI Features

Powered by Gemini 2.0 Flash

Per-Trip Insights

After every trip, 3 specific recommendations with estimated CO₂ savings and money savings in ₹.

Efficiency Score

0–100 score based on engine efficiency (60% weight) and idle time ratio (40% weight). No division by zero.

Real-Time Coaching

AI tip sent to driver every 2 minutes during active trip based on current speed, idle time, and distance.

Fleet Analytics Insights

Owner gets AI analysis of total fleet data — top emitter, overspeeding patterns, and fleet-wide recommendations.

AI Fleet Assistant

Owner chats with AI that has full context: all driver names, CO₂ values, overspeeding events, pending requests.

Hindi Language AI

All AI prompts and responses switch to Hindi when the language toggle is set to हिंदी.

Emission Formula
CO₂ = distance × emission_factor × (10 ÷ engine_efficiency) + idle_time × 0.5
Diesel: 2.6 kg/km  |  Petrol: 2.3 kg/km  |  Idle factor: 0.5 kg/min  |  Engine efficiency adjusts relative to 10 km/L baseline
